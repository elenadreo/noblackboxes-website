Governance & leadership

The board of trustees.

The No Black Boxes Foundation is guided by a board of trustees responsible for overseeing its mission, strategy, and long-term impact.

Our trustees
Elena Dreosti
Trustee & Co-founder No Black Boxes · UCL, London

Neuroscientist, co-founder of No Black Boxes, and Honorary Senior Research Fellow at UCL. Leads all course delivery and has taught hundreds of students across more than 60 countries. Brings deep scientific expertise, a long-standing commitment to open education, and direct knowledge of the organisation's mission, curriculum, and community

Peter Vincent
Trustee Veriphi Labs · London

Neuroscientist and entrepreneur. Former researcher at the Sainsbury Wellcome Centre, now founder and CEO of Veriphi Labs — an AI governance startup backed by Cambridge Angels. Brings expertise in frontier technology, scientific rigour, and early-stage venture building.

Giovanna Lalli
Trustee LifeArc · London

Neuroscientist and Director of Strategy & Operations at LifeArc. Over 20 years leading scientific strategy across Wellcome, the UK Dementia Research Institute, and medical research charities — with deep expertise in translating research into real-world impact and building partnerships across academia, industry, and government.
