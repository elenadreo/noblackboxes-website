Before you apply

Everything you
need to know.

For September 2025 — applications are now open. Email us to introduce yourself and we will send you the full application details. A new application form is coming soon.

For November 2025 and 2026 — express your interest and we will contact you when applications open.

  • Email info@noblackboxes.org with your name and which course you are interested in
  • We will send you the application form and all details
  • Selected applicants will be notified by email

Places are limited. We select based on motivation and curiosity — not prior technical experience. No background in electronics, programming, or neuroscience is required.

  • We look for genuine curiosity and desire to understand
  • We aim for a diverse group by background, career stage, and geography
  • All applicants are treated equally regardless of institution

£650 per person. Includes kit shipping worldwide, pre-recorded and live lectures, full attendance, Discord support, and certificate on completion.

Group rate: £650 for one person + kit, £350 per additional participant sharing the same kit. Maximum 3 per kit.

Through the No Black Boxes Foundation we are committed to making this accessible to everyone. If the fee is a barrier, get in touch.

  • A limited number of bursary places are available per course
  • Priority given to applicants from under-resourced institutions and lower-income countries
  • Include a brief note with your application explaining your circumstances
  • Submit a short video and written summary of your final project
  • Outstanding projects are featured on our Recognitions page and shared with our network of labs and companies
  • You receive a certificate confirming you built a complete intelligent system from first principles
  • Your NB3 kit is yours to keep and continue building with
  • Past students have gone on to internships, lab placements, and research positions

Every course is supported by TAs — researchers from leading institutions available throughout the five days via Discord. Past TAs have come from the Champalimaud Foundation, Sainsbury Wellcome Centre, Neurogears, and more.

Interested in becoming a TA? Email info@noblackboxes.org
