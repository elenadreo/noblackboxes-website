Our mission
Technology shouldn't
be a black box.

We believe everyone should understand how the technology shaping our world actually works — not just how to use it. From circuits to artificial intelligence, we teach the principles behind modern systems by building them from the ground up.

Built by neuroscientists. Inspired by students. Trusted by research institutions worldwide.

The curriculum

28 lectures.
From atoms to AI.

Every concept earned, never assumed. The only course that takes you the full distance — from a single circuit to a working intelligent system — with nothing hidden along the way.

Part 1 — Physics & Electronics 8 lectures
Part 2 — Circuits & Digital Logic 5 lectures
Part 3 — Computing & Control 4 lectures
Part 4 — Systems & Networks 7 lectures
Part 5 — Perception & Intelligence 4 lectures

Each lecture pairs its technology concept with its equivalent in the brain. Transistors and neurons. Memory chips and synapses. AI and the visual cortex. One journey. Two discoveries.

The curriculum is open source and always evolving. The NB3 kit gives you the hardware to build every concept with your hands.
