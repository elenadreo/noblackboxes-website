How connections work

Not a job board.
A personal introduction.

We don't send your details to a database. The No Black Boxes team personally connects outstanding students with the right organisations — based on your project, your badges, and your interests. Online or in person, depending on where you are.

1
Complete the course and submit your project
All pathways start here. Build something real, submit your video and summary, earn your certificate.
2
Get featured and personally introduced
Outstanding projects are featured on the Wall of Fame and shared with our partner network. The No Black Boxes team then connects you directly — with an email, a recommendation, and context about your work. Not a cold application.
3
Work, collaborate, or visit
Online project, research placement, internship, or lab visit — depending on where you are and what the organisation offers. Always something you can name on your CV.

For organisations

Join our
network.

If you are a research institute, lab, or company working at the frontier of neuroscience, AI, or hardware — and you want access to the most motivated, hands-on students in the world — we would love to hear from you.

Our students have built working robots, trained neural networks, and designed custom hardware — from scratch, in three weeks. They are not just theorists. They are builders.

Interested in our students?
Let's talk.

Whether you want to offer an internship, a research placement, an online project, or simply stay connected to our community — get in touch and we'll find the right way to work together.

We are also open to organisations who want to help shape the badge system — defining what skills actually matter in your field, and validating the badges students earn.
